Roadmap

Check out our ambitious plans: as we go, we’ll tick the points until we’re there.

  • 0% Research and Data Preparation:

    Gather relevant data for your phishing detection model, including examples of both phishing and legitimate domains.

    Clean and prepare the data, ensuring it's in a usable format for machine learning.

  • 20% Machine Learning Model:

    Develop a machine learning model for domain phishing detection. This involves creating a program that can identify whether a domain is legitimate or phishing.

    Train the model using the prepared data and fine-tune it for accuracy.

  • 40% Application Development:

    Design the user interface (UI) for your application, creating a layout and appearance that is user-friendly.

    Develop the front-end (what users see) and back-end (the logic and processing) of your application.

    Integrate the Google API to allow for domain validation, ensuring domains are legitimate.

  • 70% Testing and Refinement:

    Test your application with real users to gather feedback on usability and identify any issues.

    Address any bugs or vulnerabilities in your application, ensuring it's secure and performs well.

  • 100% Presentation and Launch:

    Prepare a compelling pitch and presentation that explains your application's purpose and features.

    Participate in the hackathon, presenting your project to judges.

    After the hackathon, launch your application for public use if it's well-received and ready for real-world use.
