  • What is the purpose of this application?

    This application is designed to detect domain phishing attempts. It helps users verify the legitimacy of website domains to protect against online fraud and phishing attacks.

  • How does the application work?

    The application uses a machine learning model to analyze a domain's characteristics and compare them to known phishing patterns. It also leverages the Google API to validate the domain's authenticity.

  • What kind of domains can the application detect?

    The application is capable of detecting potential phishing domains that mimic legitimate websites, including those used in email phishing attacks.

  • What data sources are used for training the machine learning model?

    The machine learning model is trained on a combination of publicly available datasets, including examples of known phishing and legitimate domains.

  • How accurate is the domain detection process?

    The accuracy of the detection process depends on the quality of the data and the effectiveness of the machine learning model. We continuously work to improve accuracy.

  • Is the application free to use?

    Yes, our application is free for general users. However, we may offer premium features for businesses or organizations in the future.

  • Is the application accessible to everyone?

    Yes, the application is accessible to the general public and aims to help users, regardless of their technical expertise, to identify potential phishing domains.
